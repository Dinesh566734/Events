NAVATARA

"On October 19, 2023, our institution hosted a vibrant fresher's day event, aptly titled "NAVATARA". This exciting celebration marked the beginning of a new journey for our freshers, and what a fantastic way to kick-start their academic adventure! The event was a kaleidoscope of talent, energy, and enthusiasm, showcasing the diverse skills of our students. The program was packed with an array of thrilling performances, including: Dancing,Singing and Playing instruments and many more. The event was a true celebration of creativity, teamwork, and camaraderie, providing a platform for our freshers to mingle, bond, and create unforgettable memories. The NAVATRA fresher's day will be etched in our memories forever, and we look forward to many more such events in the future!"

PROGRAMMING CLUB

"Our programming club is a vibrant community of coding enthusiasts who come together to learn, share, and create. We meet weekly to explore various programming languages, work on projects, and participate in coding challenges. Our members range from beginners to experienced programmers, and we foster a supportive environment where everyone can grow and improve their skills. We also invite industry experts to share their experiences and insights, providing valuable networking opportunities. Whether you're interested in web development, artificial intelligence, or data science, our programming club is the perfect place to expand your knowledge and build innovative projects. Join us to code, learn, and have fun!"

FILM CLUB

"Our film club is a dynamic group of movie enthusiasts who gather to watch, discuss, and appreciate a wide range of films. We screen classic masterpieces, indie gems, and contemporary blockbusters, followed by lively debates and analyses. Our members share diverse perspectives and insights, making for a rich and engaging cinematic experience. We also invite industry professionals to share their expertise and host workshops on film criticism, screenwriting, and filmmaking. Whether you're a fan of Hollywood, international cinema, or experimental films, our film club is the perfect place to explore the world of cinema and connect with like-minded individuals. Join us to watch, learn, and be inspired!"
